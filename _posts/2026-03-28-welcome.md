This is your first post written in Markdown.

Use this exact front matter pattern in every file inside _posts to control:

- `title`: headline shown on the homepage and post page.
- `date`: publication timestamp used for sorting.
- `excerpt`: the short catch shown on the homepage list.
- `thumbnail_image`: image shown on the homepage card (and at the top of the post page).

## Why this setup

The homepage automatically lists posts from newest to oldest, and each card pulls from your front matter. Keep writing in Markdown below the front matter.
